Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about schedules, items, and billing models.

Collections are delayed by exactly one day following major holidays (New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas). If your scheduled collection falls on or after the holiday, it will occur the following day. Normal routes resume the following week.
Electronics (computers, televisions, chargers, and batteries) cannot be mixed with standard curbside trash due to hazardous substances. However, you can schedule a dedicated e-waste pickup using our website contact form or drop off clean electronics at our authorized Eco City collection centers.
We provide roll-off dumpsters in 10, 20, 30, and 40 cubic yard sizes. A 10-yard dumpster is ideal for residential cleaning, whereas large home renovations or construction projects typically require 30 or 40-yard dumpsters. We deliver in 24 hours and handle necessary municipal parking permits.
