About EcoWaste Solutions

Home About Us

Pioneering the Future of Resource Management

EcoWaste Solutions was founded on a simple premise: waste should not be the end of the lifecycle, but the beginning. In an era where landfill expansion threatens ecosystems and releases greenhouse gases, our goal is to build an absolute circular economy.

We provide full-service collections and highly automated sorting services for commercial, residential, and industrial organizations. By adopting optical scanning, magnetic metal extractions, and certified composting processes, we turn waste into valuable secondary resources.

Through our green fleet and solar-powered facilities, we are actively driving environmental carbon offsets. Every collection route we plan, every container we rent, and every waste audit we perform is driven by our zero-waste philosophy.

Our Mission

To pioneer zero-waste ecosystems through cutting-edge logistics, smart recycling technology, and environmental stewardship, ensuring communities thrive and future generations inherit a healthy, clean planet.

Our Vision

To transform the waste management industry globally from a linear extraction-disposal model into a circular economy where every piece of refuse is repurposed, recycled, or converted into clean energy.

Our Journey So Far

From a local trash hauler to a multi-state premium environmental services provider, we have consistently pushed the boundaries of what is possible.

2012

EcoWaste Solutions Founded

Established with just three collection trucks and a single localized waste sorting site. Our focus was on providing transparent pricing and reliable schedules.

2015

Introduction of Composting

Launched a commercial and residential organic composting program. We began diverting food waste from local restaurants to regional agricultural facilities.

2018

Facility Robotics Integration

Upgraded our sorting plants with optical scanner robots, accelerating plastic sorting speeds by 200% and reducing cross-contamination.

2022

Transition to a Green Fleet

Purchased our first heavy-duty electric recycling trucks and transitioned fossil-fuel engines to clean compressed natural gas (CNG).

2026

250,000 Tons Landfill-Diverted

Crossed a historic milestone in resource recovery, keeping massive amounts of raw paper, plastic, glass, and metal in secondary production loops.
